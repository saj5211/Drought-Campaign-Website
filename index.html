Welcome to the Water The Earth Project!

Water The Earth, is a project focused on finding innovative ways of creating water so the number of droughts annually and the impacts associated with these droughts are reduced.

What Are Droughts?

To keep things simple, Droughts are periods of time in which different places in the world experiences a deficiency of water. Droughts are often characterized by these three types:

Meteorological Droughts

Occurs when there is a deficiency of perciptation in a specific region. Regions like California often experience this type of drought.

Agricultural Drought

Occurs when there is a low level of topsoil moisture. This type of drought usually occur inconjunction with Meteorological Droughts.

Hydrological Droughts

Occurs when there is a decrease in water availability. This usually occurs in places where the level of infastructure is fairly limited.
